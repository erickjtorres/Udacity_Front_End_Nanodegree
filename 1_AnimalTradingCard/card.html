Welsh Corgi

In the early days of Amazon, a corgi named Rufus became the unofficial mascot of the company

  • Origin: Wales
  • Life span: 12 years and two months
  • Breeds: Cardigan and Pembroke
  • Most Common: Pembroke

Corgis have been historically used as herding dogs. Their name means "dwarf dog" due to their short stature. They are most commonly found with tan fur with white markings. In addition, they are a close relative to the husky.
